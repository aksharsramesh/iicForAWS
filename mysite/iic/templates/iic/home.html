JSS SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY UNIVERSITY

Standout from the crowd

JSS STU aims to create a futuristic global university with a learner-centric ecosystem and a focus on science and technology. The University is committed to deliver high quality educational opportunities for youth and transform not only its neighbourhood but offer courses to equip aspiring youth to meet the global needs of industry in every sector. The Mission is to establish one of the world’s best universities, which is broad-based, sustainable, value driven and research-intensive and integrates nation building.

One day workshop on Intellectual Property Rights and Patenting

10 Jan 2019

Institution Innovation Council (IIC) of JSS Science and Technology University organized a one day workshop on Intellectual Property Rights and Patenting. The occasion was graced by Dr.M.N.Bheemesh, Director of National Institution of Advanced Legal Studies. Dr. Mallikarjuna, Dean - Science and Head - IP Cell, Dr. B S Harish, Convener – IIC and other distinguished dignitaries were present. The workshop witnessed a total of 120 participants. The workshop gave an overview of Intellectual Property Rights with special emphasis on patenting.

IDEATHON 2019 by Institution’s Innovation Council (IIC)

01 March 2019

Institutions Innovation Council (IIC) of JSS Science and Technology University organized IDEATHON 2019 as a mark of National Science Day. The occasion was graced by the Director of CSIR – CFTRI, Dr. K S M S Raghava Rao, Chief Guest and Prof. BG Sangameshwara, Vice Chancellor of JSS STU presided the program. Dr. Mallikarjuna Aradhya, President – IIC, Dr. B S Harish, Convener – IIC and Mr. Murugendra Bandrad, Student Coordinator and other distinguished dignitaries were present.

The council received around 65 Novel Ideas from the students of different departments. Prizes for Ideathon were announced and three ideas were chosen out of 13 shortlisted ones by the jury. A total prize worth Rs. 18,000/- was give away to top 3 ideas.

KYP- KNow your problem

15 Sep 2019 - 25 Sep 2019

Institution's Innovation Council created an interesting opportunity to address day-to-day problems which engineers could brainstorm and come up with solutions. A web portal was opened from September 15th to September 25th of 2019. There was an opportunity for an individual to post a relevant problem and also read others problems posted and upvote the best one. KYP was a huge success and witnessed over 100+ problem statements. 3 students with critical problems, bagged the highest votes and were rewarded exciting cash prizes of worth rupees 5000.

learn to lead

15 Oct 2019

Institution's Innovation Council Celebrated Innovation day on the birth anniversary of Dr. A P J Abdul Kalam i.e, on 15th October. Two young entrepreneurs , Mr. Shikar- founder of MELSTAR manufacturing systems, which manufactures precision mechanical components for companies like Bosch and Mr. Murali- co-founder of FOODBOX, a food start-up.

Both the entrepreneurs shared their journey, the challenges faced to establish in their respective markets. As both of their fields were different, they inspired the audience in distinct ways.

The gathering was graced by B.Suresh, Pro chancellor, JSS Academy of Higher Education and Research and President, Pharmacy council of India.

24 hours state level Hackathon by

IIC–JSSSTU

01 Nov 2019 - 02 Nov 2019

JSS Science and Technology University’s – Institution Innovation Council (IIC) created history by hosting the first ever 24-hour State level Hackathon in the college campus on Nov 1st and 2nd. With over 280 participants working in teams to arrive at innovative solutions, the event was undoubtedly one of the most successful ones hosted by IIC. A total of Rs. 80,000/- was given to winners.

Dr. Anurag Srivatsava, Chief Operating Officer, Society for Innovation & Development, IISC Bengaluru was the chief guest of the valedictory function. He talked extensively about “Future Readiness” and emphasized on the fact that, we don’t have a future without innovation.
